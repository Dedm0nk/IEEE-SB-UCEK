About Us

About IEEE

The IEEE is undoubtedly the most distinguished professional organization that leads technological innovation worldwide. As the largest technical professional organization globally, it plays a pivotal role in shaping the future of technology. With its mission rooted in enhancing technological innovation for the betterment of humanity, the IEEE acts as a catalyst for collaboration, knowledge sharing, and standardization. Its technical societies span a wide range of fields, including aerospace, communications, computing, and robotics, among others. Through its various activities, such as publications, conferences, standards development, and educational initiatives, the IEEE offers unparalleled resources and support to professionals across the globe, cementing its position as the go-to organization for all things technology.

About Our College

The University College of Engineering is located in Trivandrum, Kerala, and has a prestigious history spanning over two decades. It is the only engineering college under the University of Kerala and was established in 2000 with three engineering branches. The institution is located in the Golden Jubilee Complex of the Kerala University on the Kariavattom campus. The college is well-known for providing high-quality education and having an excellent faculty-student relationship. The college's location next to the Technopark, the state's IT hub, has proven to be beneficial for the students. They have access to the learning facilities in the Technopark, and there are regular interactions between the university and the industry.

About IEEE SB UCEK

IEEE SB UCEK is one of the most active IEEE communities in the Trivandrum Hub. It started in 2022, with a dedicated team and a lively community. Since then, IEEE SB UCEK has come a long way and has organized remarkable events that are certain to continue in the future. The IEEE SB UCEK is a hardworking family that aims to improve our society and nation. Around 50-100 new members join the IEEE SB UCEK community with great dedication and enthusiasm every year. Our IEEE community inspires students to embark on their journey toward the world of technology, with the potential to overcome any obstacles. We aim to produce more prominent professionals and achieve many more milestones.

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.

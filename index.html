About Us

IEEE SB UCEK is one of the most active IEEE communities in the Trivandrum Hub. It started in 2022, with a dedicated team and a lively community. Since then, IEEE SB UCEK has come a long way and has organized remarkable events that are certain to continue in the future. The IEEE SB UCEK is a hardworking family that aims to improve our society and nation. Around 50-100 new members join the IEEE SB UCEK community with great dedication and enthusiasm every year. Our IEEE community inspires students to embark on their journey toward the world of technology, with the potential to overcome any obstacles. We aim to produce more prominent professionals and achieve many more milestones

Our Vision

Our vision is to lead the way in technological advancement and positively impact society for the betterment of humanity. We strive to develop individuals into successful professionals with outstanding skills, to build a creative and innovative global community. We aim to cultivate a vibrant community of IEEE members, both women and men, who utilize their diverse talents to innovate for the betterment of humanity. We aim to inspire, enable, empower, and energize student members to enhance their technical interests. We recognize the significance of diversity in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) fields and highlight how diverse perspectives can lead to innovation and problem-solving.

Our Mission

  • Advancing Technology:
    Aims to foster technological innovation and Excellence in the advancement of humanity
  • Contribution to Science:
    To contribute more scientific facts through research and development.
  • Humanitarian Activities:
    To contribute more to empower the humanity of society
  • Women In Engineering:
    Inspiring the girls to achieve their academic interests and find a suitable career.
  • Happiness of Volunteering:
    To promote the volunteering activities in the community groups
  • Ethics and Social Implications:
    To promote ethical behavior and responsible use of technologies.
  • Skill Development:
    To ensure the versatile skills for job-ready individuals.

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.
